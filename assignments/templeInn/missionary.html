Full-Time Missionaries

We offer long-term stay suites for full-time temple missionaries and their families.

All Suites include and have access to the following:

  • Work Desk
  • Attached bathroom
  • Living Area
  • Dining/Kitchen Area
  • Washer and Dryer in the building
  • Dish Network
  • Reserved parking
  • Workout rooms within the building
  • Playroom and limited babysitting for children

Types of Suites (and amenities specific to them)

  • 1 Bedroom
    • One king sized bed in a master bedroom
    • 40inch screen TV
  • 2 Bedroom
    • One king sized bed in a master bedroom
    • One twin sized bed in its own room
    • 50inch screen TV
  • 3 Bedroom
    • One king sized bed in a master bedroom
    • two twin sized beds in their own rooms
    • 60inch screen TV
    • Access to a small balcony
